What is Citizen AI?

Citizen AI is a powerful, intelligent citizen engagement platform designed to transform how the public interacts with government services. It brings together Artificial Intelligence, real-time analytics, and multilingual support to provide a transparent, accessible, and efficient governance experience for all.

Our Mission

We aim to democratize AI and make it understandable and usable for every citizen. Our focus is on ensuring that AI is ethical, inclusive, and human-centric. Citizen AI strives to improve civic engagement, foster transparency, and drive meaningful public service innovation.

Project Description

Built with technologies like Flask, IBM Watson, and IBM Granite Models, the Citizen AI system is engineered to automate interactions, analyze public sentiment, and generate actionable insights for government departments. It enables seamless and intelligent communication between citizens and government bodies, improving trust and satisfaction.

Scenario 1: Real-Time Conversational AI Assistant

This assistant serves as the citizen's direct interface with the system. Citizens can ask questions or submit service-related queries and receive immediate AI-generated responses based on real-time natural language understanding. Available 24/7, it ensures instant support and smart automation of routine inquiries.

Scenario 2: Citizen Sentiment Analysis

By analyzing feedback and comments from citizens, Citizen AI assesses the overall public sentiment on policies and services. It uses AI to classify this feedback as positive, neutral, or negative. This information is then visualized to help officials understand what matters most to people and respond accordingly.

Scenario 3: Dynamic Analytics Dashboard

A centralized dashboard allows officials to view key metrics like service ratings, feedback trends, sentiment percentages, and user engagement statistics. By transforming raw data into charts and alerts, it enables data-driven governance and continuous service improvement.
